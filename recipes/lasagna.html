Homemade Lasagna

Do you constantly catch yourself thinking, about how you wish you could eat more tasty meals, but you have trouble cooking? Well, you can rest easy now, this recipe is so simple to make that you should already be thinking about getting a gym membership, because you will be gaining some calories from now on.

Ingredients

  • Meat

  • Onion

  • Canned Tomatoes

  • Fresh Herbs

  • Sugar

  • Spices and seasonings

  • Noddles

  • Cheese

  • Eggs

Instructions

  1. ook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente
  3. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  5. Layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.
  6. Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about half an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown.
